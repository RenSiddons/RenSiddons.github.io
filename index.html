NO COUNTRY FOR OLD MEN






There are no clean Getaways.




When Llewelyn Moss finds a briefcase full of money while hunting, he takes it for himself. Unbeknownst to him, this simple action will ensnare him in a messy conflict between the local sheriff and a serial killer by the name of Anton Chigurh. On the run and with his life ruined, can Llewelyn escape his seeming fate, or will he just be another one of Chigurh's victims.



A scorching blast of tense genre filmmaking shot through with rich veins of melancholy, down-home philosophy and dark, dark humor, No Country for Old Men reps a superior match of source material and filmmaking talent. - Variety

As stomach-churning a suspense exercise as the cinema has seen since the salad days of Hitchcock. - Premiere
